ESP Careers: be part of the best

Located in North Hollywood, CA, The ESP Guitar Company is a leading global manufacturer and distributor of quality musical instruments. ESP brands include ESP Custom Shop, ESP Original Series, ESP/LTD Signature Series, ESP USA, ESP E-II, and LTD. ESP is also the US distributor of internationally renowned acoustic-electric guitar maker Takamine, as well as other brands. We are always looking for motivated people who have a passion about music and the tools used to make it.
